History

POGLED DOO successfully performed transportation services, cargo on domestic and international
market for 23 years, to the mutual satisfaction of the company and customers.

YEAR 1992

Laying the Foundations companies

31.03.1992. established company for transportation of goods in the domestic marketplace called "POGLED DOO TRGOVISTE S RADOVNICA". It started with one truck and one worker in the small municipality of Trgoviste, in southern Serbia.

YEAR 1995

Enter international market

Due to the high demand for goods of foreign origin company introduces the possibility of transportation of goods in international traffic, increased transport capacity and expanded its activities in the import-export.

YEAR 2003

Strategic cooperation with Volvo

In order to maintain the confidence of its customers and improve its transport capacity, we decides to purchase 4 new truck Volvo FH, which also started strategic cooperation with this company.

YEAR 2010

Expanding business capacity

The increase in volume and expansion of transport capacity has led to the need for the construction of storage facilities, service and business premises, the entire business moved to a new location next to highway E75, Partizanski put BB, Vranje.
